# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards
- Be respectful and professional.
- Focus feedback on code and behavior, not people.
- Assume good intent; ask for clarification before escalating.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, hate speech, threats, or doxxing.
- Personal attacks or repeated bad-faith disruption.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or ban contributors who violate this policy.
This policy applies to issues, pull requests, docs edits, and review discussions.
